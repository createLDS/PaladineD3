Songkran Festival

April is the hottest month of the year for Thailand. To cool down, Thai citizens throw water at each other to cool down. Though the real meaning behind this is to welcome the new year in the Thailand calendar. Many tourists come to celebrate the most colourful and festive time of the year with the locals on Bangkok streets.

Never Colonized

Thailand is the only Southeast Asian country that was never colonized by an European country.

Jok

Joke is the thai version of congee which is a staple in Thai breakfast. Cooked with jasmine rice to a thick texture, seasoned with an abundance of ginger and gorund pork. Soy sauce or fish sauce can be later added for more flavour. The dish is usually topped with strands of ginger and green onion finished off with a soft boiled egg.
